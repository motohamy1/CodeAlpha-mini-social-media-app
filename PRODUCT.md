# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Visual creators, photographers, digital artists, and curators looking to showcase their work and connect with others. They need a distraction-free, premium space that respects their work and makes media consumption delightful.

## Product Purpose
A mini social media platform for sharing and exploring high-quality visual content. It provides profiles, posts, comments, and a like/follow system. Success is a fluid, high-engagement experience where creator content is the hero.

## Brand Personality
Sleek, premium, and sophisticated. The voice is quiet, confident, and refined, letting the visual media speak for itself.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered, text-heavy feeds (avoid dense paragraphs, tiny fonts, or chaotic layouts).
- Generic SaaS dashboards (avoid heavy table structures, administrative grids, and excessive management metrics).
- Chaotic neon styling (avoid over-saturated neon borders or glowing buttons that detract from the content).

## Design Principles
1. **Content is the Hero**: The interface must step back and frame the user's media. Use neutral, dark backgrounds and generous whitespace.
2. **Restrained Precision**: Every border, icon, and button must feel intentional. Avoid unnecessary UI elements and decorative glassmorphism.
3. **Tactile Delight**: Interactive elements must respond with subtle, premium micro-animations (like smooth, ease-out scaling on like buttons).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Achieve WCAG AA contrast ratio on text against dark backgrounds.
- Provide a clean focus ring styling for keyboard navigation.
- Implement `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` alternatives for all animations.
